Our Vision

Our vision for EcoNull is to create a sustainable development where we don’t interact with the global or local economy. We also work to restore, protect, and improve our environment.

Our community members are respectful, hardworking, and dedicated to cleaning the environment, removing economic barriers, and increasing human potential.

Our Pillars

  • All factories and land are publicly owned; no individual owns any item.
  • Each person has guaranteed rights to food, housing, electricity, and security.
  • Attacking another person without permission is punishable.
  • All individuals have freedom of religion.
  • No chopping trees or harming the environment.
  • All physically able people ages 16–60 must help farm for food.
  • Kids ages 6–16 receive free education (not mandatory).
  • All citizens must ensure no neighbor is left behind.
  • No trading with cash inside or outside the community.
  • All societal changes and decisions are decided democratically by the community.

Our Government

Our government is a socialist government. This means everyone is equal and everyone shares everything. If one person has something, everyone has it. Goods exist purely to fulfill human needs — not for profit.

Member Benefits

  • Free health care
  • Free farm-grown produce
  • Free housing
  • Equal rights for all members
